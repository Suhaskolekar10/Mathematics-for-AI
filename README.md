## 📘 STAGE 1: Foundation Building (Must Master)

### 1. **Arithmetic & Number Systems**

* Integers, fractions, decimals, percentages
* Factors, multiples, LCM, HCF
* Exponents and roots
* Divisibility rules

### 2. **Algebra**

* Variables and expressions
* Linear equations and inequalities
* Quadratic equations
* Functions and their graphs (linear, quadratic, exponential)

> ✅ **Application:** Forms the backbone of most ML equations, gradient descent, cost functions.

### 3. **Coordinate Geometry**

* Cartesian coordinates (x, y)
* Distance formula, midpoint formula
* Line equations (slope-intercept form)
* Understanding 2D and 3D spaces

> ✅ Used to visualize decision boundaries and vector spaces.

---

## 📘 STAGE 2: Core AI Mathematics

### 4. **Linear Algebra (🧠 Core for Deep Learning)**

* **Scalars, Vectors, Matrices, Tensors**
* Matrix operations: Addition, multiplication, transpose
* Identity and inverse matrices
* Determinants and rank
* Eigenvalues and eigenvectors
* Linear transformations and projections

> ✅ Critical for: Neural networks, embeddings, image processing

### 5. **Calculus (🧠 Core for Training Models)**

* Limits and continuity
* Derivatives (rules, partial derivatives)
* Chain rule (used in backpropagation)
* Gradient and gradient descent
* Integration (basic understanding)

> ✅ Critical for: Optimization, loss functions, backpropagation

### 6. **Probability and Statistics (📊 For Data Understanding & Inference)**

* Mean, median, mode, variance, standard deviation
* Probability rules and distributions (Binomial, Normal, etc.)
* Conditional probability, Bayes’ theorem
* Random variables and expected values
* Hypothesis testing, p-value, confidence intervals
* Correlation vs. causation

> ✅ Used in: Naive Bayes, evaluation metrics, probabilistic models

### 7. **Set Theory & Logic (💡 For Discrete Math & AI Logic)**

* Sets and subsets
* Union, intersection, complement
* Venn diagrams
* Propositional logic, truth tables
* Quantifiers

> ✅ Useful for: Formal reasoning, AI planning, symbolic AI

---

## 📘 STAGE 3: Advanced Topics (As You Progress)

### 8. **Optimization**

* Convex functions
* Cost functions and minima
* Gradient descent and variants (SGD, Adam, etc.)
* Constraint optimization (Lagrange multipliers)

> ✅ Needed in: Training ML models, tuning hyperparameters

### 9. **Information Theory**

* Entropy and cross-entropy
* KL divergence
* Mutual information

> ✅ Used in: Decision trees, loss functions, deep learning

### 10. **Graph Theory (Optional but Useful)**

* Graphs, nodes, edges
* Trees, shortest paths
* Graph algorithms: DFS, BFS

> ✅ Used in: Recommendation engines, network science, NLP (transformers)
